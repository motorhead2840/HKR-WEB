Cyber Safety Policy

Welcome to the Cyber Safety Policy of our organization. The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines for maintaining safety and security in our digital environment.

1. Introduction

This document lays out the essential practices and rules to ensure cyber safety for all stakeholders.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party service providers of the organization.

3. Policy Guidelines

  • Maintain strong passwords and change them regularly.
  • Ensure all software is updated and patched.
  • Avoid clicking on unknown links or attachments in emails.
  • Report any suspicious activity to the IT department immediately.
  • Use secure networks and VPNs when accessing company resources remotely.

4. Conclusion

Adhering to this cyber safety policy will help protect our organization and its assets. Everyone plays a vital role in our collective security.
